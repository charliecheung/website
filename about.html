About.

Hi there, I’m Charli and I am a user experience designer based out of Calgary, Alberta, Canada.

I started my UX career as many do, by transitioning from another field. My background is in Computer Science and I spent a few years as a software developer working at a AR/VR startup before realizing UX was where I could make the biggest impact.

I transitioned into a role as a UX Consultant, building products with an outstanding service experience for clients and users. Then I moved into a senior design position working in the healthcare tech industry where I learned how to solve problems creatively while working within the constraints of a rigid process full of concrete regulations.

In my current role as a Lead Product Designer, I am creating a design system, working closely with stakeholders to strategize our product roadmap, and building the foundations of a design team.

A couple years ago, I decided to start a YouTube channel talking about design. What started as a way to talk about something that none of my friends had an interest in, became a platform for me to share my life and career with an amazing community of passionate designers.

Having spent most of my life practicing martial arts, I like to spend my free time as a taekwondo and tai chi instructor. I’m also an avid journaler and a pet parent to my dog, Bun. My latest passion has been in homemaking.
