about

Hi, I'm Charlie and I am a user experience design consultant in Calgary, Alberta, Canada.

As a software developer turned ux designer, I act as the bridge between designers and developers in creating beautiful and usability driven applications.

My responsibilities on a team is a unique blend of consultant, designer, and developer. At the core of it all, my mission is to provide the most outstanding service experience for clients and users. This prioritization of service design has led me to developing expertise in user research, usability testing, business and data analytics, and user interface design.
