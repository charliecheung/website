about

Hi, I'm Charlie -a user experience designer and freelancer working in Calgary, Alberta, Canada.

As a software developer turned ux designer, I act as the bridge between designers and developers to create beautiful and usable client applications.

My role on a project is a unique mix of consultant and designer. The consultant in me does the research, provides the recommendations, and guides clients to a solution that fits their budget and ensures they hit their success target. The designer in me creates the visuals for a beautiful system to delight clients and end users.

As a consultant, my goal is education and empowerment. I want to share what I've learned and what I'm learning! Follow me on my journey by connecting with me on any of my social networks.
