About.

Hi, I'm Charli and I am a user experience designer based in Calgary, Alberta, Canada.

As a software developer turned UX designer, I act as the bridge between designers and developers in creating beautiful and usability driven applications.

My responsibilities on a team are a unique blend of consultant, designer, and developer. This is due to my expertise in user research, usability testing, business and data analytics, and user interface design.

Currently I am working on creating a design system for our product.

In my free time, I love video journalling and sharing my life as a UX Designer on YouTube. Doing so has allowed me so many opportunities including mentoring designers from around the world and working with brands developing tools that further UX research.
