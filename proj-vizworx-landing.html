vizworx landing page


project overview

This is a mock up design of the VizworX company landing page. When creating this, I wanted to keep in mind the three areas of expertise VizworX offers: development in augmented reality, virtual reality, and data visualizations. The website highlights VizworX's geospatial system, GeoViz and a child website for VizworX AR. This page is meant to serve as a landing page for a scroll down single page website.
