It is what we do that defines us.

I create with intent.

The way I think and design is based around systems. Answering the questions of how and why something can grow, expand, and evolve is what drives my design process.

My background as a software developer, love for aesthetics, intuitive user experience, and conquering challenging business goals build the foundation to all of my work.

You can find me on YouTube educating aspiring designers, mentoring individuals on Superpeer, designing products for my shop, and freelancing as a design consultant.
