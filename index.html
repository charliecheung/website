Hi – my name is Charlie and I am a UI/UX designer and software developer based in Calgary, AB, Canada. What I do is a bit different from other software developers because I put the people first. It’s not enough to have efficient applications but they must be usable – easy to learn and easy to use. That’s where the UI/UX comes in. By polishing the user interface and improving user experience, I make software that people want to keep using, accomplishes the task, and looks great too!
My passion for programming and my creative outlook inspires me to create solutions – beautiful, simple, and elegant. This passion ignites my desire to expand and improve my skills so I’m always learning new frameworks, languages, and design techniques.

Be it for front-end development or software development, I’m about applications for everyone. Following an agile development plan, I design prototypes while factoring software constraints and requirements to develop using test driven development. This open communication style between the team and the client lets me develop and design the best application.

When I’m not busy designing and developing, I am a taekwondo and tai chi instructor, board game enthusiast, avid reader, and baker. Keep scrolling down my page to see more about what I do and view my portfolio.
